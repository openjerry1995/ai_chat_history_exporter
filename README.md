# AI Chat History Exporter

Save your AI conversations permanently. Export single chats or your entire history from Grok, Gemini, ChatGPT, and Claude — all in clean Markdown format.

[![Chrome Web Store](https://img.shields.io/badge/Chrome-v2.1.1-blue?style=flat-square)](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)

## Features

- Export single chats or your entire conversation history
- Clean Markdown format, readable forever
- Works with all major AI platforms
- 100% local — your data never leaves your device

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Current Chat | All History |
|----------|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| **ChatGPT** (chatgpt.com) | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Claude** (claude.ai) | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Grok** (grok.com) | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Gemini** (gemini.google.com) | ✅ | ✅ |

## Privacy

This extension runs **100% locally**. No data is collected, stored, or sent to any server. Your conversations never leave your computer.
